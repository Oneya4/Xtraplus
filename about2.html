Our People

Our people are at the heart of our Success. They form the key component to our continued good performance and growth. "Our People Make the Difference" which underscores the value we place on the people that form the backbone and driving force of the company. We understand that our growth as a corporation rests on the growth of the individuals who make up the corporation.
XtraPlus Technologies Ltd was established and registered in Kenya on 7th January, 202 by the Founders George J.O. Ojiro and Ruth W. Kagima.
The company’s initial aim was to successfully integrate innovation, agility and latest ICT best practices in business. We are currently focusing to be recognized as a leading Microsoft Partner, Cyber and Data Security Provider, Physical Security Provider, Enterprise Software Advisor (ESA) for mid and large size business organization in Africa and beyond.
We strive to do our best to ensure that all ICT related problems for our clients in business environ ment are solved and fixed, in the best possible way!
The first company Chairperson is George Kagima Kariuki.
The XtraPlus Technologies Ltd Founders are committed to establish a company with an outstanding legacy and longevity to serve beyond the Founders and chains of generations to come!
